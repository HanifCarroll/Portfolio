About Me

I started my journey into the world of coding on March 19th, 2017. I spent hours that day reading about different languages, what they were used for, and their relative ease of use for beginners. After a lot of deliberation, I came to choose Python, and I absolutely loved it!

3 weeks went by, and I was feeling fairly comfortable with the basics of the language: I could write loops and understand how they work, how to read and write from files, webscraping, etc. However, I didn't see this as being my calling, my final goal; at least not for the time being. Being satisfied with knowing enough for the time being to make timesaving scripts for my personal use, I looked to web design, and I saw so many incredible projects being made with stunning visuals; all this really spoke to me. This is what I wanted to be a part of.

Later that week I joined FreeCodeCamp and I've been making my way through the curriculum there. I aim to spend a lot of time studying this art so that I can get started on a career that I can look forward to waking up every morning for!
